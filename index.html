Foundations of Dynamic Systems

A set of self-contained, offline interactive lessons on first-order systems — the simplest dynamical systems, where one stored quantity relaxes toward an equilibrium. Every lesson follows the same arc: pick the state, build the equation from a balance law, solve it, and watch it respond. Tank, circuit, coffee cup, or hydraulic cylinder — it is one equation wearing many costumes.
