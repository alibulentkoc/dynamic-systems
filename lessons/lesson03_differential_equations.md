# Lesson 3 — Differential Equations, Read Physically

:::{admonition} About this lesson's demos
:class: note
The slope field and phase portrait below are a **self-contained HTML/JavaScript file** — no
libraries, no build step — computing every arrow and trajectory live. Open it directly:
<a href="../differential-equations.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><code>differential-equations.html</code></a>.
:::

You built the equation of motion in Lesson 2. This lesson teaches the skill of **reading** it —
seeing an ODE as a field of instructions ("given where you are, here's where you head next") rather
than something to crack. Two interactives carry the idea: a slope field for first-order equations,
and a phase portrait that shows a second-order solution as a shape in state space.
