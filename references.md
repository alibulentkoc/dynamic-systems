# References & Further Reading

Curated, beginner-friendly next steps (added to as the course grows):

- Katsuhiko Ogata, *Modern Control Engineering* — the standard thorough reference.
- Norman Nise, *Control Systems Engineering* — approachable, example-rich.
- Karl Åström & Richard Murray, *Feedback Systems* — free online, excellent intuition.
- Herbert Merritt, *Hydraulic Control Systems* — the classic for fluid power dynamics.
