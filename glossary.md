# Glossary

A living glossary; terms are added as each lesson introduces them.

**Dynamic system.** A system whose output depends on the history of its inputs, not just
the present input — it stores energy and therefore has memory.

**State.** The minimum set of numbers describing "where a system is now" such that, with
future inputs, its future is determined. Often position and velocity.

**Static system.** A system whose output depends only on the present input.

**Time constant (τ).** The characteristic time that sets how fast a first-order system
responds; larger τ means slower.
